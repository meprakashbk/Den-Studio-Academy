Common Questions

Do I need prior experience to enroll? +
No prior experience is needed. The course is designed to take you from beginner to production-ready, covering all fundamentals from the ground up.
What DAW do I need? +
The course is DAW-flexible, but we primarily teach concepts applicable to Ableton Live, FL Studio, and Logic Pro. We'll guide you on the best option for your goals.
Are classes live or pre-recorded? +
Both! Classes are held live online so you can interact with instructors in real-time. All sessions are also recorded so you can revisit material at any time.
How much time should I commit per week? +
We recommend setting aside 8–12 hours per week — including live classes, practice sessions, and working on your projects.
What do I need to get started? +
A laptop or desktop with a DAW installed, a pair of headphones or studio monitors, and a stable internet connection. More detailed equipment guidance is provided on enrollment.
Is there a certificate upon completion? +
Yes — students who complete the 13-week program receive a certificate of completion from Den Studios Academy.
